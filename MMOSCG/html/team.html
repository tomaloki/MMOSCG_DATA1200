About us

We are a freelance company consisting of eleven employees. We specialize in web design and coming up with innovative solutions for modern problems. Our employees are all first-year computer engineer students at Oslomet. Our vision as a company is to provide the world with solutions that make information accessible for everyone. Our company is still small, but we believe that through smart design and innovative ideas, the gap between different people will be bridged. We were invited by the Norwegian government to submit a proposal for a mobile app. The app will allow Syrian children to continue learning, even though they are living in crisis. The app is free.
